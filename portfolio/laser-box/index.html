Description

This piece is my attempt to capture the effect of a laser cutting through fog, complete with cryptic messages in the background. The casing is 3D printed from sparkly pink bioplastic, and is brought to life by the following components:

  • Adafruit Trinket M0
  • SparkFun Qwiic Alphanumeric Display - Pink
  • Grove Water Atomization Module
  • SG92R Micro Servo
  • 1" x 1" square mirrors
